About Us

We are a non-profit 501(c)3 corporation providing quality dance instruction to people with special needs while Promoting Awareness in the community through the Joy of Dancing!

Dance Classes

There are a variety of classes to choose from based on what your need is. There are four days of classes per week: Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays. There are four different locations, too: St. Catherine's School, Premiere Dance Academy, Village Dance Studio, and Welborne United Methodist. Some classes are split for ages, and some are split for dance experience. There are different types of classes that we offer, too, like tap, ballet, and company classes. Classes last from 30 minutes to an hour long. You can also sign your child up for private lessons and duets. Classes range from $325 - $650 per dance year.
