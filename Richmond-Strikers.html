Richmond Strikers

About Us

The Richmond Strikers are a non-profit organization founded/established in 1975 that is committed to providing the Greater Richmond area with an effective, dynamic and progressive athletic experience offering a place for children to play their chosen sport. At the Richmond Strikers, we are devoted to providing a developmentally appropriate structure for thousands of children ages 4-18 to experience the game of our offered sports at every level. With our expansion into other sports, we now have over 4,500 players in our travel and recreation programs. We are committed to providing quality instruction and programming as well as first class facilities.

We are an organization dedicated to:

  1. Growth and support through sports of the Richmond, Henrico, Chesterfield, Hanover and surrounding area communities
  2. High standards in character
  3. Quality instruction
  4. Player development

The Richmond Strikers are dedicated to and understand that the true value of long-term success comes through the development of both technical skills and team play over any individual game result. We also know and appreciate that success comes in many forms, and through many sports. This is why we are working diligently to expand our offerings to encourage more of our families to enjoy and learn through their chosen sports with us.

We are proud to be an established US Soccer Development Academy Club and to be recognized as one of fifty Nike Premier clubs in the country.

Mission Statement

Mission: The Strikers aim to provide unsurpassed individual development, family enrichment and community connection through team sports.

Vision: To be the best athletic club with the greatest sports opportunities.

Our Brand: The Strikers brand is based on the principles of intelligence, innovation, and respect. We believe teamwork and the pursuit of excellence with enthusiasm are the mechanisms through which our values are lived.
